Stage 2 · Aligned (Pre‑GTM)

This tool lives between your System Building Playground snapshot and the Messaging Alignment Lab. It turns your internal language into a simple “this is for me” client lens.

How this connects

System Building Playground gave you your ecosystem and model. This page distills that into one core client-facing message. The Messaging Alignment Lab will take this line and match it to channels and real-world stories.

Neurospicy-friendly use tips

  • Do one field at a time. You can leave and come back.
  • Bullet fragments are fine. You can smooth later.
  • Use words you’d say out loud to a friend.
  • If you get stuck, start with the struggle, not the who.
  • It’s okay to make different versions for different client types.

Internal vs Client Lens

Your internal snapshot is for you — it holds the full system. This client version is for them — it helps them see “this is for me” in a few seconds. You need both.

Shared Language · Aligned

Internal snapshot
Your full ecosystem + model notes from the System Building Playground.
Client lens
A one-glance version that helps a real person recognize themselves quickly.
Who
The specific kind of person this offer is for (life, work, context — not just “entrepreneurs”).
Struggle
How their problem shows up in daily life, in their body, energy, and time.
Readiness note: If naming “who” and “struggle” feels impossible or wildly vague, it may mean you’re still in a Rooted or early Aligned season. You can still experiment here, but it’s okay to pause and get more clarity with System Building Playground or Rooted-level work first.
